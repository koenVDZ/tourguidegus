Just a sample of the attractions we are offering

Our goal is making sure your stay in Rio de Janeiro will become an unforgettable Experience.

"One day trip in Rio"

Visiting the main and must-see sites. That covers the Statue of Christ, riding the train all the way to the top, through the National Park, part of Tijuca Forest. We also visit Sugar Loaf, riding the cable car to the top of the two hills. On top of it, we visit the Metropolitan Cathedral and a drive through the city center, seeing some old and new all mixed. A stop for lunch of your choice or follow my recommendation for a traditional and local spot.

*Tickets and lunch are not included.

"Wedding in Rio!"

Make your dream come true. Get married on a paradisiac beach ,in a tropical forest or elsewhere.Tell us what your dream is or leave it all up to us and we'll put it all together.

"Walking Tour"

This is a very authentic tour as we can walk all over certain areas of interest. This can be dosed according to your pace. We can drive and walk , drive again and walk more or just drive ,park and then do the whole tour walking. This can last 4/6 or 8 hours( 3/4 or 6 miles of walking) with a strategic stop for lunch at a traditional and local place. It covers the historical area , old town , the Olympic Boulevard ,Santa Teresa or elsewhere , in order to suit your interests.

*Tickets and lunch are not included.

"Food Tour"

A Gastronomic fun experience in Rio where you can taste several different and typical fruits , finger foods , pastries , dishes and drinks of Brasil , with a special focus on the traditions of Rio. On this tour we walk in a farmer s market for fruits tasting , sugar cane juice with "Pastel" , then we walk and grab several different types of food throughout the city ,stopping for lunch to eat the famous "Moqueca" or "Feijoada" and we finish up the day at the trendy "Bar da Urca" where locals have pastries and beer ,right by the bay area ,with a magnificent view.

"Hang gliding in Rio"

Hang gliding in Rio. Enjoy this unique, thrilling and very safe experience with the top pilots of Rio. Pick up at the hotel, fly for about 20 minutes, over the forest , the water , jumping off a 550 m hill and landing on this white sandy beach . We book you with one of our 5 different best pilots and partners in Rio. Drop off at your hotel.

"Cooking Class"

This specific tour gives you a practical class on how to make some traditional dishes of Brazil, with a special touch on Rio. With the best English-speaking Chefs. Learn, since the first steps, how to prepare an authentic Feijoada, a Moqueca, Farofa and the famous and delicious Caipirinha. And the best: at the end everyone sits to eat and drink, celebrating the final result.
